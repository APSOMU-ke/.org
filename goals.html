SHORT TERM
GOALS

Promoting Political Science

The organization aims to promote and advance the study of Political Science among students. We foster an environment where academic excellence is not just a goal, but a culture.

SCROLL
Short Term Goal

Conferences &
Seminars

Through organizing events such as conferences, seminars, and workshops, we facilitate academic discussion and provide opportunities for students to engage directly with Political Science scholars and practitioners.

Short Term Goal

Resources &
Mentorship

We provide resources and support for students to excel in their studies through dedicated study groups, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities.

LONG TERM
GOALS

A Leading Organization

To be a leading organization in the field of Political Science, through the promotion of research and scholarships among its members.

Long Term Goal

Policy &
Partnership

Set to influence policy and contribute to the development of Political Science as a discipline within higher education. We foster collaboration and partnership with other organizations and institutions to advance the field.
