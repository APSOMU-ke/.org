What made me to lobby for the formation of Association

I personally took my time to interact with different students pursuing political science and public administration from different levels that is from 1st year to 4th year,

It happened that majority had some questions which had never been answered, some were like:

  1. How will this course benefit me after doing my 4 year Degree.
  2. How will this course benefit our family and community at large being that it's not well known to the public.
  3. What are some of the key areas we will be doing our attachmens
  4. Who are some of the prominent people who did political science as a course and how did they benefited from such?

This questions kept on ringing on my mind and I said that I should now come with an initiative that will help solve all this problems and come up with a permanent solutions to our fellow students.

I was further involved in different case study,. I was trying to study how University of Nairobi political science students Association has been operating and some of the benefits they have enjoyed through this Association, I also tried to access keenly how international Association of political science students works and some of the benefits it has driver to it's members.

After collectie research and I decided to lobby for the formation of Association of political science students of MOI UNIVERSITY (APSOMU). I shared the idea with my classmates, Chair of Department, political science lecturers, Dean of students and today I'm proudly to announce that they were convinced with my initiative and as we speak we have a functioning Association for political science students.

MISSION

To create a sense of community and provide opportunities for political science students to explore the major both academically and professionally.

VISION

To provide Political science students with a variety of opportunities and experience to help further develop their academics and career goals.
