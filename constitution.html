Background

APSOMU - the Association of Political Science Students at Moi University - is founded on unity, academic excellence, and civic engagement. It serves as a platform for students passionate about governance, policy, and leadership.

Mission Statement

To promote intellectual inquiry, responsible leadership, and active citizenship through academic forums, debates, mentorship, and collaborations.

Core Values

  • Democracy and accountability
  • Inclusivity and equality
  • Integrity and respect for diversity
  • Academic and professional growth

Purpose

APSOMU aims to:

  • Foster dialogue among political science students.
  • Provide mentorship and networking opportunities.
  • Organize conferences, seminars, and workshops.
  • Support members' academic and career development.

Leadership & Structure

Executive Committee and Working Committees guiding our mission.

  • President
  • Vice President

Working Committees

  • Focused groups for specific initiatives

Membership

Open to full-time Moi University students majoring or minoring in Political Science. Members must register, agree to the constitution, and pay a membership fee.

Leadership Positions

APSOMU is governed by an Executive Board comprising:

  • Chairperson: Official spokesperson and event organizer.
  • Vice Chairperson: Deputy leader and member liaison.
  • Secretary General: Record keeper and agenda coordinator.
  • Treasurer: Oversees finances and funding.
  • Organizing Secretary: Maintains legal documents and club records.
  • Welfare Secretary: Handles member welfare and social activities.
  • Debate Chairperson: Leads debates and intellectual forums.
  • Publicity Secretary: Manages branding, communication, and social media.
  • Class Representatives: Act as links between members and officials.

Elections

Held annually by secret ballot. All registered members are eligible to vote and run for office without discrimination.

Governance and Amendments

Meetings

Officials meet biweekly to discuss organizational matters. Minutes are recorded and shared.

Financial Management

APSOMU operates as a non-profit association. Funding comes from student associations or departmental support. Donations require member approval.

Risk Management

APSOMU is a registered student association but operates independently from Moi University.

Amendment Process

Changes to the constitution require unanimous Executive Board approval, followed by a two week public comment period before ratification.
